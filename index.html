Keywords: Norwegian viking, strong background in music composition for film and video games, love to learn, world traveler, culture explorer, hobby webdev, hobby painter and dog lover

Wonder if I can get the job done? Here's professional me:
I used to work freelance in London for 10 years, which made me develop iron skin. As a composer you are mainly tailoring music to match someone else's vision. Much like with UX design, it's crucial to put your ego aside and empathise with the client's and business' needs and pain points to work towards a common objective.

As a designer my mission is to make work that actually makes a difference to people’s lives, whether it helps making a small every day activity easier or something more meaningful. Through 2019 I grew to be a generalist who handles both UX and UI; defining design strategy, solving complex UX problems, quickly prototyping and iterating, and delivering the product at pixel perfection level. I'm especially fond of team work, user research and animating micro interactions and value flexibilty and new challenges.
