Tiffin Café
- Designing an
E-commerce site

DESCRIPTION: Tiffin is a family run café in the center of Lisbon serving plant based food made with love. They wanted to increase their online visibility with an E-commerce site, but on their own terms making sure the style felt like them.

ROLE: We were three UX designer doing the user research, then my role was to take care of the competitive and business research as well as wireframes, ideating, branding, design, prototypes and preparing assets for the developer ..


TIMELINE: April- May 2020 using the Agile method and delivering protoypes for mobile, tablet and desktop

01 Challenges

We were three UX designers doing the research in the beginning phase. My team mates, Ines Ferreira and Frederico Dias were absolutely great to work with and together we came up with some cool ideas. For the design phase we each had a go at creating a version of the design so Tiffin had multiple options to choose from. The challenge was to make something that felt authentic to Tiffin and that they felt represented them well.

01

Problem, How can we help them increase their online precense in a way that they feel correctly represented?

02

The goal was to design a web page that had their core values of honesty, authenticity and community at heart.

03

The solution was a simple and straightforward web page reflecting all the values that were important to the café
