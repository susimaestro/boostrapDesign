Fresh eyes testing the
NOS Play Web App

I was curious to test how users who had never seen the NOS Play website would interact with it the first time they used it. Based on what the NOS clients said they struggled with in the interviews and surveys, I made up a list of tasks for the new users to do, asked them to think out loud as they were going through it, screen recorded the sessions and finally asked them how they think it went in the end.

User 1 - Finding any thriller movie
It took 9 clicks.
The user said the menus keep changing. In terms of placement and content the menu was very confusing and not very intuitive.

User 2 - Navigating from series section to movie section
It took 11 clicks.
The user thought it was strange you have to go back to the home in order to go from series to movies. A direct navigation would make more sense to them.

User 3 - Browsing the series section

The user thought it was very annoying to have to go through alphabetical categories and said it required a lot of their patience.


What are the strenghts, weaknesses, opportunities and threats of NOS PLAY? Who are the competitors? What are they doing well and what can they improve on? And what can make NOS Play unique on the market? From the user research we could see that Netflix and HBO were the biggest competitors.

Understanding the Business goals
and Competition

STRENGHTS

They are the leading entertainment group in Portugal and come across as approachable and relatable to their customers. Their clients love their content varierty on NOS Play.

WEAKNESSES

Most of their customers use Netflix and HBO to stream content as they find the NOS Play app confusing, not very intuitive or engaging and difficult to navigate through.

OPPORTUNITIES

Their competitors are catering heavily to the consumer who doesn't know what to watch, so how can we cater to the movie geek who knows exactly what they want to watch as well? Can we incorporate playlists and connect our social media accounts?

THREATS

Netflix and HBO invest heavily in their research department that results in tailored content for their customers. How can NOS do the same on their budget?

Findings from research

Positive feedback


Great Content Variety

The majority of the users mentioned how happy they were with the content, offering a wide variety of international movies and rare Portuguese movies



Good Brand Values

NOS portrays the image that they really care about their customers

Negative feedback


Confusing Interface

The NOS customers find the interface difficult to navigate through, confusing and not very intuitive



Difficult Menus

The menu position and the content changes depending on what site you're on

 


Alphabetically ordered content

The users prefer having tailored suggestions show up and if they want to see a specific movie they'd rather use the searchbar.



Not userfriendly or intuitive

The platform takes time to learn and get used to. Most of the users said they gave up on it
