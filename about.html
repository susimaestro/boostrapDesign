Keywords: UX/UI Designer, Norwegian born viking, based in Lisbon, strong background in music composition for film and video games, love to learn, world traveler, culture explorer, hobby webdev, hobby painter and dog lover

Wonder if I can get the job done? Here's professional me:
I used to work as a freelance composer in London for 10 years, which made me develop iron skin. As a composer you are mainly tailoring music to match someone else's vision. Much like with UX design, it's crucial to put your ego aside and empathise with the client's and business' needs and pain points to work towards a common objective.

My UX/UI journey started in 2019. As a designer my mission is to make work that actually makes a difference to people’s lives, whether it helps making a small every day activity easier or something more meaningful. Through 2020 I grew to be a generalist who handles both UX and UI; defining design strategy, solving complex UX problems, quickly prototyping and iterating, and delivering the product at pixel perfection level. I'm especially fond of team work, user research and animating micro interactions and value flexibilty and new challenges.

Currently I am working freelance on short term projects and would love to find a permanent remote position with a company that cares about people.
