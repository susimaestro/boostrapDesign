NOS PLAY
- Redesign &
On-boarding

DESCRIPTION: NOS is the leading entertainment group in Portugal and NOS Play is one of their streaming services. The client wanted to create an onboarding experience for their mobile and web app in hopes to engage their customers to use the service more. After the user research it was understood a redesign was also in order to suit their needs


ROLE: UX/UI designer responsible for conducting user research, development, creating the onboarding experience and the redesign.


TIMELINE: May- June 2020 using the Agile method and delivering prototypes

01 Challenges

The challenge was tackled with the use of Agile methodology with a user-centered approach. Extensive user, competetive and business analysis was made in order to come up with design solutions to better the service.

01

The problem was that NOS customers were not really using the app because they found the app tedious, confusing and difficult to navigate through.

02

The goal was to make the service more engaging, intuitive and improve menu navigation

03

Solutions included static menus with simplified options and layout + engaging features like customizable lists and the option to connect to social media

04-01 Low-fidelity prototype

Time to start putting the ideas to life in a low-fidelity prototype starting with the mobile version.

On the right is the first low-fidelity prototype of the mobile Onboarding experience.

Changes after first test:
- Adding the option to go back in case of error.
- The option to change genre was also put before picking movies so the movie option would correspond to your genre preference.

04-02 Mid-fidelity prototype

To the left is the first Mid-fi prototype. I made a prototype on the platform Useberry, which allows you test your design with your users and shows you a video of where they clicked. Very useful to see what's intuitive to the users!

Changes that were made:
- Elements were made smaller
- Rectangles changed to NOS style
- Adding go back options
- Adding 'skip' option

To the right is the final mid-fidelity version that the users had very little trouble with. It was tested with 25 people who said they found the prototype easy to navigate through and intuitive.

04-04 Main features

- Static menu for a better navigation experience
- Onboarding experiencfe to engage the user and tailor the content from the start
- A filter search option to also cater to the user who knows what they want to watch
- A customizable list option so the user can organize movie playlist in whatever fashion they want
- An option to connect with your social media so you can see what your friends are watching
- No hidden hamburger menus, all the options are laid out in plain sight at all times on the static menu bar
- Comment, share and review content with friends
